The FC Barcelona culture carries its own unique identity that can be used as a form of symbolism and pride. Here, we see the club's most important features.

  • Slogan
  • Badge
  • Anthem

Slogan

The club's motto “Més que un club” translates to “More than a club” which is a common catalan phrase that FC Barcelona uses to declare that they are not just a sporting organization but a family of like minds supporting and fighting for the same thing. The five principal values of FC Barcelona are respect, effort, ambition, teamwork, and humility.

Badge

The club's crest is one of the most famous among the world of football. The badge represents the club's roots. The top left portion is commonly mistaken for England's national flag but it is actually the St. George’s Cross which represents the patron saint of Catalonia. The top right area represents the colors of the catalonia flag. The bottom half represents the blaugrana colors of blue and red (garnet). Lastly, The clubs initials lie in the middle of the badge and a small football rests over the Barca colors.

Anthem

The official Barcelona anthem is called the “Cant del Barça”. It was written in 1974 by Josep Espinas and Jaume Picas for the club’s 75th anniversary.
